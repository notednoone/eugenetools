Eugene Men's Social Group

Welcome to the Eugene Men's Social Group! We're a friendly, laid-back community of men in Eugene who are looking to make new friends and connect over shared interests. Whether you're joining us for a fun trivia night at a local pub or just hanging out over some great food at one of our regular grill meetups, you're sure to find good company and great conversations. Come for the events, stay for the friendships!
